What is Drop about?

Drop is a mobile game where players work to heal the planet by restoring ecosystems, such as planting trees and cleaning up oceans.

How does Drop make a real-world impact?

For every in-game action, such as planting trees or cleaning up trash, Drop partners with environmental organizations to plant a real tree or remove plastic from our oceans. We aim to bridge the gap between gaming and real-world environmental conservation.

Which organizations does Drop partner with?

Drop collaborates with a variety of established environmental NGOs focused on reforestation and ocean cleanup efforts. We regularly update our list of partners to ensure we’re contributing to meaningful projects globally.

Is there a cost involved in supporting these environmental efforts?

Drop is free to play, and a portion of the revenue generated through ads, in-app purchases, or donations goes directly to funding environmental projects. Players can also choose to donate directly through the app if they wish to further support these causes.

How can I get involved or support beyond playing the game?

Beside playing Drop, you can support our partnered NGOs through direct donations via the game's interface, volunteer for environmental causes, or share Drop with friends to increase its impact.
