About Us

We are a small, independent studio with a big mission: to create a mobile game that empowers players to make a real difference in the fight to protect our planet. Our game, Drop, is more than just entertainment—it's a way for players to actively participate in the repopulation of endangered flora and fauna, and the restoration of ecosystems around the world. Our passion for the environment drives everything we do. Through Drop, we aim to raise awareness about the urgent need for conservation while providing a fun, engaging platform for players to contribute. With every tree planted, every ocean cleaned, and every species saved, we’re bringing hope back to nature. Together with our community of players and our dedicated partners, we believe that even small actions can lead to big changes. Join us on this journey to help heal the planet, one ecosystem at a time.
